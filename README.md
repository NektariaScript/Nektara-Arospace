# Nektara-Arospace
A fictional aerospace company website built with HTML and CSS as part of my Web Development Internship at Codveda Technologies.

A five-page website for Nektara Aerospace, a fictional private aerospace agency created as part of a web development internship project.

Rather than presenting itself as a company focused on rockets, satellites, or commercial space travel, Nektara Aerospace exists to celebrate humanity's curiosity and our timeless desire to explore the unknown. The site is designed as an immersive, editorial experience — closer to standing beneath a twilight sky than browsing a corporate product page.

Live Pages
Page	File	Purpose
Home	index.html	Cinematic landing page introducing the agency's vision and belief that exploration begins with curiosity
Missions	missions.html	Fictional past, present, and future missions across the solar system and beyond
Research & Technology	research.html	Innovative aerospace technologies and exploratory initiatives
Our Team	team.html	The fictional scientists, engineers, and astronauts behind the agency
Contact	contact.html	A contact form and correspondence details

Tech Stack
HTML5 — semantic markup, five static pages
CSS3 only — no JavaScript, no build tools, no dependencies
Google Fonts — Fraunces (display serif) and Jost (body sans-serif), loaded via @import

Design System

Concept: the feeling of standing beneath a vast, star-filled sky just after dusk — quiet, dreamlike, and full of wonder.
